About

KIRA HELBERG is a Senior Analyst at Flywheel Partners, a Healthcare Communications Agency in New York City. She graduated in 2016 from UCLA with a B.A. in Economics and recently started the Columbia University Engineering Coding Bootcamp program in January 2019.

She became interested in web development after growing frustrated with innefficient and non-user-friendly design some websites and applications. She aims to use her new web development skills to create applications that have a simplistic and easy-to-use interface to help improve user interaction.

Besides coding, her interests include: drawing, painting, photography, cooking, baking, and listening to true-crime podcasts (she's a Murderino!).
